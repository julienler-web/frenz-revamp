Designed for children from birth to school age
Great for newly arrived French families in Auckland

Primary School

Ages 5–11
Bilingual Primary Programmes

Our primary bilingual units are embedded in public schools across New Zealand. Children are taught the full NZ curriculum in both French and English, achieving true biliteracy across all academic subjects.

18 hours per week of education in French
6 years of continuous bilingual learning (Years 0–6)
All teachers are native French speakers, NZ-registered
Children don't need to know English to enrol
ESOL support available at most partner schools

📋 Who is this programme for?

The programme works best for children who already have some basis in French — we teach in French, not about French. Some schools accept international students; others accept NZ citizens/permanent residents or work visa holders only.

Eligibility

Is This Programme Right for Your Family?

Our bilingual programmes are designed for children with an existing foundation in French. The goal is to learn in French, not to learn French from scratch.

✅

Great for families where French is spoken at home

Native French-speaking families, Franco-kiwi families, or families with significant French exposure.

✅

Your child doesn't need to know English

Most schools provide ESOL support (English for Speakers of Other Languages) for new arrivals.

📅

Enrolment timing varies

Most schools accept children year-round; some only start new students in February. Contact us to confirm!
