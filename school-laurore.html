The Dawn — A New Beginning

Aurora means "dawn" in French — representing a fresh start, new horizons, and the light that bilingualism brings to young minds.

Haeata — Community at Heart

Haeata Community Campus is an innovative, community-driven school with a progressive approach to learning — perfect for bilingual education.

Christchurch's Youngest Unit

L'Aurore is one of Frenz's newest units — passionate, growing, and bringing South Island intermediate students into French language excellence.

About Nadège

Nadège was born in Lyon and moved to New Zealand in 2009 after working as a marine biologist in the Pacific. She settled in Christchurch with her Kiwi partner Sam and had three children — two of whom are now at Haeata L'Aurore.

Deeply passionate about science education, she was part of the founding team that created L'Aurore and has been instrumental in shaping its educational philosophy. She believes bilingual education is one of the greatest cognitive gifts parents can give their children.

"Every day is a new dawn at L'Aurore. Our students rise with more knowledge, more confidence, and more connections to the world."
