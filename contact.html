🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need to know French to enrol?

Yes, our programmes work best for children who already have some French. The curriculum is taught in French, not designed to teach French from scratch.

Does my child need to know English?

No! Children don't need English to start. Most partner schools provide ESOL (English for Speakers of Other Languages) support.

When can my child start?

Most schools accept children year-round. A few only accept new students at the start of Term 1 (February). Contact your school of interest to confirm.

Are there any fees?

Frenz programmes are embedded in public schools, so education is free for NZ citizens and permanent residents. International students may pay fees — please enquire with the specific school.
