Who We Are

More Than a Language School

Frenz is a not-for-profit organisation helping public schools across New Zealand open and operate quality French-English bilingual programmes. Our units are fully integrated within each school — students participate in all aspects of school life while gaining true biliteracy.

🔄

Smooth Transition

Children who don't speak English yet find it easier to settle in and make friends — language is no longer a barrier.

📚

True Biliteracy

Students achieve native-speaker proficiency in all academic subjects in both French and English.

🌟

Academic Advantage

Research shows early exposure to multiple languages gives children a lasting cognitive and academic edge.

The Science

Why Choose Bilingual Education?

Decades of research confirm that bilingualism isn't just a life skill — it's a brain superpower.

🧠

Lifelong Learning

Children exposed to two languages have brains more attuned to learning. Studies show slower cognitive ageing and higher employment rates in adults with bilingual capacities.

💞

Greater Empathy

Bilingual children learn to read social cues to decide which language to use. This builds exceptional skills in perspective-taking and emotional intelligence.

🌍

Cultural Richness

Language carries culture. Growing up bilingual means children become bridges between communities, gaining a richer worldview from an early age.

🚀

Career Advantage

Bilingualism opens doors globally. French is the world's fifth most spoken language, widely used in diplomacy, business, and the arts.

🤝

Community Belonging

Frenz families become a tight-knit community. Parents share multicultural values and many lifelong friendships are formed through our programmes.

📈

Higher Literacy

Former Frenz students consistently demonstrate above-average literacy levels well into intermediate and high school, praised by their teachers.
