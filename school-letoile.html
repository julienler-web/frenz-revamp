What Makes Us Special

Unit Highlights

🌊

North Shore Community

Nestled in the beautiful North Shore suburb of Takapuna, close to beaches and parks — a stunning place to learn and grow.

👨‍🏫

Passionate Native Teachers

Our classroom is led by native French-speaking teachers registered to teach in New Zealand — authentic, fluent, committed.

🤝

Growing Family

One of Frenz's newer and fastest-growing units, with a warm and welcoming community of Franco-Kiwi families on the Shore.

🌐

Bilingual Achievement

18 hours per week of French instruction across all subjects means children achieve true biliteracy by the end of primary school.

About Émilie

Émilie is originally from Paris and lived in London for 12 years, building both her career and her bilingual family. She has two children and moved to Auckland in 2022 with her Kiwi husband to start a new chapter.

In 2024, her older son joined the Étoile du Nord unit and she became the proud parent representative — a role she takes on with enormous enthusiasm and dedication. She brings a London-sharpened energy and Parisian flair to everything she does for the unit.

"Moving to Auckland was a leap of faith. The Frenz community made us feel at home from day one."
