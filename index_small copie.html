Who We Are

Empowering Bilingual Education

Bilingual education offers children the opportunity to become proficient in all academic subjects in both languages.

Why Choose Us

The Benefits of Bilingual Education

Smooth Transition

Bilingual education provides children who do not speak English a smooth transition to your new life in New Zealand. It is easier and quicker for them to make friends because there are no language barriers.

Native Proficiency

With bilingual education, your children will have native speaker proficiency in all academic subjects in both French and English. It's more than just maintaining a minority language.

Academic Advantage

Studies in language development show that young children with exposure to more than one language at an early age are at a distinct advantage throughout life.

Lifelong Learning

The more that children can take advantage of new concepts, the more in tune their brains will be to all learning throughout life. Some studies show that the brain ages more slowly in bilingual adults.

Empathy & Social Skills

Bilingual children have demonstrated a head start in tests of perspective-taking and theory of mind - both fundamental emotional and social skills.

Community

Our Programs

French Programmes Across New Zealand

From early childhood through intermediate, we offer comprehensive bilingual education pathways.

0-5 Years

Les Petits Lascars

Weekly French playgroup in Auckland. These playgroups provide parents and children a place to play, learn and socialise in a 100% French environment.

📍 Auckland
5-11 Years

Primary Bilingual Units

Frenz supports the bilingual programmes of 5 public primary schools across New Zealand. Each school provides children with 18 hr/week of education in French for 6 years.

📍 Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch
11-13 Years

Intermediate Programmes

French-English bilingual programme is offered in Kowhai Intermediate School in Auckland Central and Haeata Community Campus in Christchurch for Years 7 & 8.

📍 Auckland & Christchurch
