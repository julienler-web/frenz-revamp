About Raphael

Raphaël grew up in Lyon and spent ten years working in the wine industry in France and Australia before settling in Christchurch with his New Zealand wife Claire. They have three children, two of whom are currently in La Découverte.

He joined the Frenz committee three years ago and became unit representative last year. His passion for community, culture, and language makes him a natural fit for this role — and he's determined to see La Découverte grow and thrive in the South Island.

"Frenz gave my children the gift that no school alone can give — a second language and a second culture, lived every single day."
