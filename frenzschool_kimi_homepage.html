Who We Are

Not-for-Profit
Bilingual Education

Frenz is a not-for-profit organization helping public schools across New Zealand open and operate quality French-English bilingual programs.

The bilingual units are fully integrated within each school, with students participating in all aspects of school life. This unique approach ensures that children don't just learn a language—they live it.

Bilingual education offers children the opportunity to become proficient in all academic subjects in both languages, giving them a unique advantage in our interconnected world.

Why Choose Us

Why Bilingual Education?

Research shows that bilingual children develop stronger cognitive abilities, better problem-solving skills, and enhanced cultural awareness.

Smooth Transition

Bilingual education provides children who do not speak English a smooth transition to your new life in New Zealand. It is easier and quicker for them to make friends because there are no language barriers.

Native Proficiency

With bilingual education, your children will have native speaker proficiency in all academic subjects in both French and English—not just maintaining a minority language.

Academic Advantage

Studies in language development show that young children with exposure to more than one language at an early age are at a distinct advantage throughout life.

Lifelong Learning

The more that children can take advantage of new concepts, the more in tune their brains will be to all learning throughout life. Bilingual brains age more slowly and employment rates are higher.

Empathy & EQ

Bilingual children follow social cues to determine which language to use. They demonstrate a head start in perspective-taking and theory of mind—fundamental social and emotional skills.
