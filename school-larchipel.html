Unit Highlights

🌊

Heart of Auckland

Located in vibrant Bayfield Primary right in Auckland Central — a diverse, dynamic community where cultures naturally blend.

👨‍🏫

Native French Teachers

All teachers are native French speakers who are fully registered to teach under the NZ curriculum. Authentic language, every day.

📚

French Library On-Site

A dedicated French-language library stocked and maintained by Frenz gives students access to French literature from the very start.

🤝

Thriving Parent Community

L'Archipel has one of the strongest parent volunteer communities in the Frenz network — a second family for many families.

About Florence

Florence grew up in Paris but has spent most of her adult life in places around the world — Exeter, London, Geneva, Dubai, and Hong Kong — before settling in Auckland with her Kiwi husband Richard.

She is passionate about cultures and languages, which is exactly why she's so deeply involved in Frenz. Her older children Sophie and Lucas are both in L'Archipel, and young Félix (not yet two!) is already very familiar with the L'Archipel family.

"Language is the key to unlocking the world. I want my children to grow up with as many keys as possible."
