Our Story

About Frenz

A volunteer-driven, not-for-profit organisation dedicated to bilingual education in Aotearoa New Zealand since 1994.

Our Mission

Opening Doors Through Language

Frenz is a not-for-profit organisation helping public schools across New Zealand open and operate high-quality French-English bilingual programmes. Our bilingual units are fully integrated within each school — students participate in all aspects of school life while developing true biliteracy.

We are run by parents, for families. Every volunteer on our committee is passionately committed to giving children the gift of two languages — and to building a warm, multicultural community where everyone belongs.

What We Do

How Frenz Supports Schools & Families

We go far beyond simply connecting children with French classes. Frenz provides comprehensive, ongoing support to ensure every unit thrives.

📚

French Learning Resources

We provide all French-language learning materials and maintain a dedicated French library on-site at every partner school.

💰

Financial Support

Frenz applies for grants and provides financial support to help schools set up new bilingual units and sustain existing ones.

👨‍👩‍👧

Volunteer Power

Our volunteer parents support classrooms, manage libraries, fundraise, and find French teaching assistants (stagiaires) to help teachers.

🌐

Promotion & Advocacy

We maintain relationships with partner embassies and actively promote bilingual education across New Zealand online and at events like the French Festival.

🚀

New Units Support

We guide and support parent groups who want to open a bilingual programme in their community, from initial planning to launch.

🏆

LabelFrancÉducation

We help schools apply for and maintain this prestigious seal of quality, awarded by the French Ministry of Education to validate French-language learning outside France.
