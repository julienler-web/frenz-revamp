How to Choose a School

All Frenz partner schools are fully integrated within their host public school. Select the school closest to you, then follow their individual enrolment process. Most schools accept children throughout the year — but some only take new students in February. You can also follow each school's activities on their Facebook page. If you need help deciding, get in touch with us and we'll guide you through the process.
