What Makes Us Special

Unit Highlights

🌿

Named After NZ's Golden Flower

The kowhai is one of New Zealand's most beloved native trees — golden, proud, and deeply rooted. Just like our students.

🎓

Seamless Transition Year

We bridge primary and high school, maintaining bilingual learning during the crucial transition years from Year 7 to Year 8.

🇫🇷

Advanced French Curriculum

At intermediate level, French goes deeper — students explore literature, history, and culture en français with our experienced team.

🌏

Intercultural Exchange

Kowhai connects with Francophone schools internationally, giving students authentic pen-pal and exchange opportunities.

About Jérôme

Jérôme came to Auckland from Bordeaux in 2010 to work in the tech industry. He married his Kiwi wife Aroha, and they have two teenagers — both of whom have been through the Frenz system from primary to intermediate level.

He joined the Kowhai Intermediate committee because he wanted to give back to a programme that shaped his kids so profoundly. As a software engineer, he's also been instrumental in modernising how Frenz communicates with parents and the wider community.

"Watching my children switch effortlessly between French and English — and between two cultures — is the greatest gift Frenz has given us."
