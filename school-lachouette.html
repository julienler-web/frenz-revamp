About Estelle

Estelle grew up in France and met her Aucklander partner Mike in Wellington after backpacking through New Zealand in 2012 — "just for a year." They have two daughters, and the youngest is currently in Ruru La Chouette.

Estelle started as a teacher assistant six years ago and is now in her second year as a full-time teacher. Their whānau is deeply grateful for Frenz's work in uniting French and Kiwi parents and teachers to raise well-rounded "Friwi" children.

"Frenz is more than a school programme — it's a community that helps us raise our children with both their cultures fully embraced."
