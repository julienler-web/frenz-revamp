School Overview

A unique learning environment in the heart of Christchurch

Haeata Community Campus is a publicly funded school in Christchurch's Aranui suburb, just 10 minutes from downtown and a few minutes from New Brighton.

Area School Advantage

Haeata is an area school, catering from new entrants to Year 12. The bilingual programme welcomes students from New Entrants to Year 8.

Haeata offers free school hot lunches to all students, eaten together in the cafeteria during lunch break—similar to the French "cantine" tradition. Children may also bring lunch from home if preferred.

Built for 1200 students but currently home to around 650, Haeata provides ample space to learn and grow in state-of-the-art new buildings.

Enrolment Process

Begin your journey

Enrollments are open for citizens, permanent residents, and work visa holders. Children from all over Christchurch can enrol at L'Aurore and start at any time of the year—the special programme is not zoned.

1

Express Interest

Contact us at francaisachch@gmail.com to advise your interest in enrolling your child.

2

Language Evaluation

The school will contact you to schedule a language evaluation for your children.

3

Receive Confirmation

Haeata will score your children's application against priority points and confirm acceptance.
